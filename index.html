About the Artist

I'm an independent artist who loves bringing original characters to life through calm and futuristic worlds.
My art often blends gentle colors, quiet emotions, and small details that tell a bigger story.
I'm currently developing my own story project, a universe that grows with each illustration.

Every drawing is a small piece of it.

Ninjin

A fox Kaiju searching for a Guardian pure enough to share his power.

 Centuries later, he found Kitsune, the girl who would unknowingly become his guardian.

Calm yet playful, Ninjin carries both warmth and sorrow, a fire that protects and consumes at once.

Kitsune

A bright, impulsive girl who hides a quiet loneliness behind her smile.

 She lost her memories in a tragic storm, and was saved by Ninjin, the fox kaiju who would become her companion.

She now lives between two worlds: ordinary life at a cafe, and a forgotten bond she slowly begins to rediscover.

Yuki

A model admired for his elegance, unaware of the fragments of power in his soul.

 He was once a guardian, now a fallen one, wandering between light and memory.

His calm gaze hides a forgotten past, and the quiet ache of something lost long ago.

Kai

A feline Kaiju who once fought as a samurai before turning into a legend.

 Gifted with the power to control others' actions, Kai uses his abilities with unmatched mastery.

Though once noble, he now drifts through life as an opportunist, charming, cunning, and dangerously self-assured.

Taka

A young man from Antihill City who survives by fixing, building, and stealing.

 Taka lives alone, taking odd jobs to earn credits, beneath his carefree smile lies quiet determination, a will forged by loss and survival.

His life changes the day he accepts a mysterious offer from the Yurei.
